OUR MISSION

As parents ourselves, our mission is to ensure parents and caregivers have the tools and knowledge they need to raise happy, healthy babies. After witnessing the struggles of parenting children with feeding issues, and seeing millions of other families experiencing similar problems, we pledged to take a stand. Our goal is to improve the precious first years of every child through the use of technology. Our next-generation baby bottle, combined with our holistic parenting app, is meant to change the lives of parents and children, and build trusting relationships between all parties involved in the child’s care. Join us on the next generation of parenting, backed by science.

FOCAL POINT

Our primary area is to enhance the trust factors and serve best baby bottles to parents and caregivers (baby feeder). Also, to be the best feeding bottle and go-to choice for parenting. Pay only for what you use. Subscription module is divided into premium and EMR based. Premium subscriptions is provided to parents with complimentary services such as yoga and exercise section for both mom and baby, and selected audios. Seamless connection between parents and caregivers. Integrated EMR system helps to connect with the paediatricians on timely basis.
